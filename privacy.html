Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in connection with The Hidden Nation Awakens website and social media content.

Last updated: December 2025

1. Who we are

“The Hidden Nation Awakens” is an educational media project that produces documentary-style video content and related materials about history, lineage, and culture. Online properties may include this website, TikTok, YouTube, and other social platforms.

For questions about this policy, you can contact: royaltproductions@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

This project is content-focused and does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data. However, the following information may be collected or received:

  • Basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages viewed, collected automatically by hosting providers or analytics tools.
  • Interaction data such as likes, follows, comments, or messages on TikTok, YouTube, or other platforms, according to those platforms’ own policies.
  • Voluntary information that you send by email or contact forms, such as your name, email address, or questions.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so it can be removed.

3. How information is used

Any information we receive is used for limited, legitimate purposes, such as:

  • Responding to questions, feedback, or collaboration requests
  • Understanding general audience trends and improving content
  • Protecting the security and integrity of our accounts and services
  • Complying with legal obligations or responding to lawful requests

We do not sell personal data or use it for targeted advertising beyond what is provided by the social platforms themselves.

4. Cookies & third-party services

This website and our content may use cookies, pixels, or similar technologies through third-party providers (such as hosting, analytics, or embedded video players). These tools help measure traffic, performance, and engagement.

TikTok, YouTube, and other platforms are operated by third parties with their own privacy policies. Your use of those services is governed by their terms, and any data they collect is subject to their policies, not ours.

5. How information is shared

We generally do not share personal information except:

  • With service providers such as hosting, email, or analytics platforms that help operate the project
  • When required by law, court order, or to respond to legal process
  • To protect our rights, security, or the safety of others
  • In connection with a reorganization or transfer of this project’s digital assets

6. Data retention & security

Information is kept only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data; however, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights & choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data-protection laws, such as the ability to:

  • Request access to personal data we may hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact royaltproductions@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

8. International audiences

Content may be viewed from locations around the world. By accessing the website or social channels, you understand that information may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions that may have different data-protection laws than your home country.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in content, technology, or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the website or content after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: royaltproductions@gmail.com.
